Lost but
never
alone.

Adularia is the meeting point of two artists — Nicolás Vidal (Parallel) and Santiago Perea (Numen) — shaped by different places, converging on the same frequency.

Named after a mineral whose inner glow only reveals itself up close. A project rooted in the belief that the dancefloor is still one of the few places where nothing needs explaining.

The music moves through dark, stripped-back techno, broken beats, and the nostalgic weight of UK garage — built for soundsystems that make your chest feel the low end before your ears do. Each set is a single continuous arc. A gradual descent into something that feels simultaneously familiar and unreachable.
