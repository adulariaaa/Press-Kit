About

Adularia is the meeting point of two artists — Nicolás Vidal and Santiago Perea — shaped by different places, converging on the same frequency. A project rooted in the belief that the dancefloor is still one of the few places where nothing needs explaining.

Operating in the space between memory and dissolution, the project draws from slow-burning electronic textures, industrial weight, and the kind of nostalgia that doesn't reach for anything specific. Each set is a single continuous arc — a gradual descent into something that feels simultaneously familiar and unreachable.
