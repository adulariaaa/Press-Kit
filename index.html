Lost but
never
alone

Named after a mineral whose inner glow only reveals itself up close, Adularia is the meeting point of artists Nicolas Vidal (Parallel) and Santiago Perea (numen) shaped by different places, converging on the same frequency. A project rooted in the belief that the dancefloor is still one of the few places where nothing needs explaining.

Their work explores the tension between movement and stillness. Repeating echoes that shift their trajectory before dissolving back into drift. Their pieces function as transitional states where energy and contemplation coexist. Adularia shifts between dark ambient, Techno, UK Garage, Drum & Bass, and experimental electronic music. Morphing styles, dense textures, and a melancholic state, Adularia converges their sound into the dancefloor.

This philosophy extends into their monthly series "Delivery Mixes", each built through exchanges with artists and scenes across different geographies. Each volume is conceived as a continuous narrative where selection and transition work as one. The series has received support from artists such as Gabo Barranco (AAAA), Concepción Huerta, Lyo XS, Octoptic, Benfika, Aurora Halal, A. Fruit, Regal86, and Leslie García (Microhm), among others.

With independent releases and support across editorial playlists, Adularia understands electronic music as a space where meaning is experienced; a place where sensation remains.
